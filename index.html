About

Designed at the edges of every discipline.

Graphic design. Product design. Game development. All of it.

I'm Colm Lenehan, a graphic and product designer based in Ireland. I run ACE Productions, a tech-media company focused on global-scale innovation in gaming, film, AI, cloud tech, and interactive media.

Currently working with Pelican Party and building PlayerCloud, crossplay infrastructure for PC gaming with free and paid tiers including CloudBoost and CloudBoost+.

My setup: Mac mini M4, Nintendo Switch, PS5, Xbox Series X, Meta Quest. I design for the people holding those controllers.
